Tips

When playing the game you can click on any empty spot and the last color peg that was used will be placed in that spot. If you click on a spot that already has a peg in it then it will not replace that peg, instead it will change the active peg to that color.
Example: If you drop a white peg and then drop a black peg clicking on one of the empty spots will place a black peg in the empty spot. If you now click on the white peg that was dropped and then click the last empty spot Extreme Mind will place a white peg in that spot.

Playing on Extreme will give Extreme Mind the ability to leave some pegs empty. If you are playing the game and drop a peg in a spot that you meant to leave empty you can easily remove the peg by either double clicking it or just right clicking the peg. When a peg is removed the avtive peg becomes the color of the peg removed.
Example: You first drop a black peg, then a blue peg, next a red peg, and finally a yellow peg. Right clicking (or double clicking) the black peg will remove it. Now right clicking (or double clicking) the blue peg will remove it as well. Clicking on one of the two empty spots now will place a blue peg in that spot. Now you could also click the red peg and then click the last empty spot and a red peg will be placed in that spot.
